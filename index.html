Our Craft Story

WOODEN VOGUE was founded by two mariners, Wooden Vogue transforms a passion for the sea into expertly crafted, handmade wooden pieces. Drawing inspiration from nature and the ocean, we create unique works using traditional woodworking techniques.

WOODENVOGUE was born from a passion for transforming raw timber into objects of beauty and function. Each piece tells a story of patience, skill, and reverence for nature's materials.

Our artisans combine traditional woodworking techniques with contemporary design sensibilities, creating heirloom-quality pieces that bring warmth and character to modern spaces.
